# Data-Analyst-project1

Insurance Analysis
This project is an Insurance Analytics Dashboard designed to track and analyze business performance across different sales activities.
It provides insights into revenue, meetings, opportunities, and sales pipeline stages.
The dashboard helps stakeholders make data-driven decisions, improve sales efficiency, and monitor overall branch performance.

Key KPIs
1 Target vs Achieved vs Invoice
→ Compares planned targets with actual performance and generated revenue.
2 Cross Sell / New / Renewal Achievement %
→ Shows performance across different business categories.
3 No. of Meetings by Account Executive
→ Tracks productivity of each executive.
4 Yearly Meeting Count
→ Shows growth in client engagement over time.
5 Invoice Count by Executive
→ Measures contribution of each executive in revenue generation.
6 Total Opportunities & Open Opportunities
→ Represents sales pipeline strength.
7 Opportunity by Revenue (Top 4)
→ Highlights highest revenue-generating opportunities.
8 Stage Funnel by Revenue
→ Shows revenue flow across sales stages and identifies drop-offs.
9 Product Distribution
→ Displays revenue/share by different insurance products.

Overall, this dashboard gives a complete view of sales performance, helping improve conversion rates and business growth 📈.
